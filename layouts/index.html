Mission & Impact

Our Mission

Protecting forest biodiversity by equipping firefighters with real-time data. Our dashboard enhances situational awareness, reducing response times and environmental damage.

Hybrid Technology

Smart Solution

We use AI-powered visual intelligence combined with a resilient sensor mesh. Integrating YOLO algorithms and LoRa/Mesh connectivity ensures detection even in remote areas.

Reliability & Validation

Our Validation

Tested in night visibility, rain, and fog. With high precision and low latency, our system ensures reliability and minimizes false alarms in critical situations.

Our Partners

Instituto Superior Técnico

Portugal’s leading engineering school, renowned for its cutting-edge research and innovation in science and technology. IST provides a robust academic environment, equipping students with the knowledge and expertise to tackle real-world challenges in various engineering disciplines.

Civil Protection

Civil Protection is responsible for coordinating actions to prevent, prepare for, and respond to emergencies and disasters. Its mission is to ensure the safety of populations by promoting community resilience and reducing risks. By collaborating with various entities, Proteção Civil plays a vital role in protecting lives, property, and the environment.

Fire Department

The firefighters are dedicated to protecting lives, property, and the environment through fire prevention, emergency response, and rescue operations. They play a critical role in ensuring public safety and providing assistance during natural disasters, accidents, and other emergencies.

City Council

The City Council is responsible for managing the city’s urban development, public services, and cultural initiatives. It aims to create a sustainable, inclusive, and innovative environment for residents and visitors alike, ensuring the continuous growth and modernization of Portugal’s capital.
